A tutorial to learn what SEO is and how to implement it

Description

A fun little guide I created that mainly goes over technical & on-page SEO. It does not go over link building and social media tools. Ultimately, this guide is meant for people (like myself) who get stuck on improving a websites traffic and ask themselves "How can I make this better?"
