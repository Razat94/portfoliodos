Description:

I created this portfolio for the sole purpose of making my projects more accessible. Over the years, I refine my portfolio to overcome any shortcomings it may bare. Below are some of the iterations my portfolio has gone through.

Iteration #1

Wireframe of 1st Iteration on Desktop. Click to expand
Wireframe of 1st Iteration on Mobile. Click to expand

This was my very first iteration way back from 2017! As you can tell, everything was extremely simplified:

  • All projects were listed on 1 page
  • Home page & About Us page are on the same page.
  • Footer was unneccessary since links could've been placed in the header.

Overall, this page has hopefully showed a brief look of how my portfolio used to look.

Iteration #2

This was the second iteration of my portfolio. Although this was labelled as a single iteration, there have been many changes. The immediate changes were:

  • Homepage has every project listed; clicking on a project will take you to its relevant project page
  • Home page & About Us page are on seperate pages.
  • Footer has been removed & have been placed in the header.

Having said that, there were also a lot of subtle changes that were added. For example, here were some of the main ones:

  • Bootstrap 4.1 has been implemented
  • Better SEO - Pages now have a page title & a page description
  • CSS animations were added

This iteration was what I originally had in mind when I thought of the word "online portfolio".
