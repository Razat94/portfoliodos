College Emails

See wireframes for college e-mails

Description:

This is an ongoing project of mine where in my free time, I attempt to design templates for popular higher education e-mail campaigns. I intend to make more wireframes in the future so stay tuned.

Currently, I've created 4 different types of e-mail campaigns:

  • Campus Store
  • Online Courses
  • Newsletter
  • Event invitation

E-mail 1: Campus Store

Wireframe of e-mail inviting students to check out the campus store

This was intended to be a simple e-mail to notify students and faculty to come check out the campus store.

For this project, I used these links as references:

  • UCLA Store
  • USC Store
  • CSUN Store

E-mail 2: Online Courses

Wireframe of e-mail telling users to check out online courses

This wireframe was to simply notify students about some of the online programs the university offers.

References:

  • UCLA Extension
  • USC Online
  • CSUN Online
  • Udemy

E-mail 3: Newsletter

Wireframe of e-mail telling users to check out latest news

The backbone of any higher education instituation is to provide local events for students and alumni to attend to. This wireframe informed students about upcoming news.

References:

  • UCLA Recreation
  • USC Calendar
  • UCSD Main Website
  • UCSD Calendar
  • CNBC
  • NPR

E-mail 4: Event Invite

Wireframe of e-mail telling users to check out an event

This wireframe notified students about an upcoming event.
